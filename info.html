EXIT Family includes seven events on seven different and amazing locations in five countries in the Balkan region: Sea Star Festival in Umag (Croatia), EXIT Festival in Novi Sad (Serbia), Sunland Festival in Primorsko (Bulgaria), Revolution Festival in Timisoara (Romania), Sea Dance Festival in Budva (Montenegro), Ada Divine Awakening in Ada Bojana (Montenegro) and No Sleep Festival in Belgrade (Serbia).

Under the symbolical slogan of EXIT 2.0, EXIT team marked the beginning of its new era in 2021, announcing numerous innovations in all areas of its activities. The home of EXIT festival, the medieval Petrovaradin Fortress, was in the focus of the global music scene, with more than 40 stages and zones on EXIT hosting some of the biggest music stars of today! Moreover, several selected EXIT acts from the previous two decades, whose performances have earned them a firm place in the festival’s history, also made a stop at the Fortress.
